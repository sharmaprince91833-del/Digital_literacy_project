Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students
Use strong and unique passwords for every online account.
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
Avoid clicking on suspicious links in emails, messages, or social media.
Do not share UPI PINs, OTPs, or banking passwords with anyone. ✅
Always verify the payee details and transaction amount before making online payments. ✅
Keep your devices updated with the latest software and antivirus patches.
Limit sharing of personal information on social media platforms.
Log out from accounts when using public or shared devices.
Report cybercrime incidents immediately via:
National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
Helpline: 1930
