social-media-checklist

✅ Do’s
Use privacy settings to control who sees your posts.
Verify information before sharing or reposting.
Post content that reflects positively on your personal and professional image.
Engage respectfully in discussions and debates.
Take breaks to maintain a healthy balance between online and offline life.


❌ Don’ts
Share personal information like phone numbers or addresses publicly.
Post inappropriate or offensive content.
Respond angrily or impulsively to disagreements online.
Follow or engage with suspicious links or accounts.
Share unverified rumors or false information.
